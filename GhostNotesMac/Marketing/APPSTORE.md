### Pitch
GhostNotesMac is your personal reading sanctuary — a quiet, beautiful space where your saved articles wait without the noise of the modern web.

### Features

**Your Library, Organized**
- Save articles from anywhere with a single click
- Beautiful library view with quiet library aesthetic
- Progress tracking so you never lose your place

**Reading Mode — Distraction-Free**
- Clean, focused reading experience
- Georgia serif font for comfortable long-form reading
- Adjustable font size (14–28pt)
- Toggle between serif and sans-serif
- Works offline — read anywhere

**Quick Reads**
- 3-minute read mode highlights shorter articles
- See reading time estimates at a glance
- Mark articles as read or archive them

**AI-Powered Insights**
- AI-generated summaries surface key points
- Highlights capture the essence of every article

**Sync & Privacy**
- Syncs across your devices
- Your reading data stays private
